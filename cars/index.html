BUGATTI CHIRON

The Bugatti Chiron is a mid-engine two-seater sports car
designed and developed in Germany by Bugatti Engineering GmbH
and manufactured in Molsheim, France, by French automobile manufacturer Bugatti Automobiles S.A.S.

FERRARI

The Ferrari 488 (Type F142M) is a mid-engine sports car
produced by the Italian automobile manufacturer Ferrari.
The car replaced the 458, being the first mid-engine Ferrari to use a turbocharged V8 since the F40.

LAMBORGINI

The Lamborghini Aventador (Spanish pronunciation: [aβentaˈðoɾ]) is a mid-engine sportscar
produced by the Italian automotive manufacturer Lamborghini. In keeping with Lamborghini tradition,
the Aventador is named after a Spanish fighting bull that fought in Zaragoza, Aragón in 1993.
